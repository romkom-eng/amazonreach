Why Excel Spreadsheets Fail

Most K-Brands execute inventory planning on Excel. But Excel doesn't account for:

  • Seasonality Spikes: Like Prime Day or Black Friday.
  • Lead Time Variances: Shipping from Busan to LA isn't always 25 days.
  • Sales Velocity Changes: What if an influencer mentions your product tomorrow?
