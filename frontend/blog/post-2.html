The "Dimensional Weight" Trap

Amazon charges based on whichever is greater: actual weight or dimensional weight. Many K-Beauty brands ship in beautiful, bulky boxes that are mostly air. You are essentially paying Amazon to store air.

Hidden Fees You Probably Missed

  • Long-Term Storage Fees: Inventory older than 180 days gets penalized heavily.
  • Removal Orders: Disposing of unsold stock costs money.
  • Placement Service Fees: Splitting shipments to multiple warehouses vs. paying for single-destination placement.
