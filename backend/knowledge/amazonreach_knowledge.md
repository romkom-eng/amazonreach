## Common Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I sign up?**
A: Click "Start Free Trial" on the homepage. You'll get 14 days free on the Starter plan. No credit card required.

**Q: Can I change my plan later?**
A: Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied.

### Integrations

**Q: How do I connect my Amazon seller account?**
A: Go to Settings → Integrations → Amazon. You'll need your Seller ID and MWS Auth Token from Amazon Seller Central.

**Q: Can I connect multiple Amazon accounts?**
A: Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans support unlimited accounts.

**Q: What if I hit my integration limit on the Starter plan?**
A: You'll need to upgrade to Professional to add more integrations. We'll notify you when you approach the limit.

### Features

**Q: What's the difference between basic and advanced analytics?**
A: Basic analytics shows current month data with simple charts. Advanced analytics includes historical trends, forecasting, comparison reports, and export functionality.

**Q: How does AI inventory optimization work?**
A: Our AI analyzes your sales history (last 30-90 days) to calculate sales velocity and predict when you'll run out of stock. It recommends optimal reorder quantities and timing.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, but only on Professional and Enterprise plans. You can export in CSV or JSON format.

### Support

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Use the chat widget in your dashboard or email support@amazonreach.com.

**Q: When will I get a response?**
A: Starter: 48 hours, Professional: 24 hours, Enterprise: 4 hours.

**Q: Can I schedule a call?**
A: Enterprise customers get a dedicated account manager. Professional and Starter users can request calls for urgent issues.

## Amazon Selling Basics

### FBA vs FBM

**FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon)**:
- Amazon stores and ships your products
- Prime eligibility
- Higher fees but easier logistics
- Recommended for most sellers

**FBM (Fulfillment by Merchant)**:
- You handle storage and shipping
- Lower fees but more work
- Good for large/heavy items
- Requires reliable logistics

### Fees

Common Amazon fees:
- Referral fee: 8-15% of sale price
- FBA fee: $2.50-$5+ per unit (varies by size/weight)
- Storage fee: $0.75-$2.40 per cubic foot per month
- Long-term storage: $6.90 per cubic foot (12+ months)

*AmazonReach automatically calculates all fees in your profit reports.*

### SEO Best Practices

**Product Title**:
- Include main keywords
- Keep under 200 characters
- Format: Brand + Model + Key Features + Size/Color
- Example: "Brand X Wireless Earbuds - Noise Cancelling, 30H Battery, IPX7 Waterproof - Black"

**Bullet Points**:
- 5 bullets max
- Start with key benefit
- Include specifications
- Use customer language

**Description**:
- Tell a story
- Include use cases
- Address common objections
- End with strong call-to-action

## Troubleshooting

### Dashboard Not Loading
1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try incognito/private mode
4. Contact support if issue persists

### Data Not Syncing
- Marketplace integrations sync every 1 hour
- Manual refresh available in Settings
- Check API connection status
- Verify credentials are still valid

### Low Stock Alerts Not Working
- Ensure you've set reorder thresholds
- Check notification settings
- Verify email address is confirmed
- Professional+ users: Check AI optimization is enabled
